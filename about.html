Our Vision

The NC Xi Chapter of Sigma Alpha Epsilon shall endeavor to create and maintain the highest possible level of brotherhood. Its members shall exemplify The True Gentleman, as set forth by John Walter Wayland, and pursue personal growth socially, intellectually, spiritually, and physically; in order that the chapter exhibit leadership, scholarship, integrity of character, and service. Nationally, Sigma Alpha Epsilon's mission is to advance the highest standards of friendship, scholarship, leadership and service for our members throughout life.
